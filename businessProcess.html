Business Process

Global Business Processes

Making numbers and statistics come alive to tell a story is the specialty of Global Business Processes (GBP). Through mathematical modeling, applied scientific knowledge and planning, GBP can lower costs on everything from supply chains to store construction. We are literally the science behind everyday low prices.

GBP is composed of engineers, statisticians and project managers who come together to make what powers Innovative Solutions run better. We believe that small details can make a big difference when the goal is repeatable outcomes. GBP is where ingenuity, hard work and ambition are helping to save people money and live better.

Global Shared Services

The responsibilities and rewards are big at Global Shared Services. This division of Innovative Solutions manages operations such as accounting and finance, audit and recoveries, controls and compliance, centralized store hiring and operational procurement. By providing back-office services like these, GSS supports our stores, clubs, distribution centers and the Home Office by allowing operators to focus on growing sales and serving our customers.

At GSS, you have the ability to impact Innovative Solutions global business and community. We have associates working at nine sites, located in five different countries, all seeking to provide simplified, reliable and compliant shared services to support our global business objectives of saving people money so they can live better.

Global Integrated Processes

Beyond our associates delivering on our mission in our stores, a team of Innovative Solution associates works behind the scenes to make better possible on a larger scale. In Global Integrated Processes, we work with Innovative Solution businesses and divisions around the world to simplify our administrative back office processes by reducing redundancy and maximizing efficiency. As part of the team, you'll look at every aspect of process, data and technology standardization and ask, "What can I do to make it better?". When we make things better, we operate for less and pass those savings on to our customers. Whether it's leveraging technology to simplify processes in Finance, Real Estate, Human Resources, Facilities Management or Sourcing, the mission is the same - saving people money so they can live better.
